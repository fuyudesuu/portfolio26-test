Things I do for fun

Hobbies

Beyond the screen, these are the things that keep me curious and energized.

Photography

Street & landscape photography across Southeast Asia

I picked up a camera in 2019 during a solo trip through Vietnam and never put it down. These days I shoot mostly on a Fujifilm X-T5, chasing golden hour in alleyways and rice fields. My work leans toward moody street photography and wide landscape compositions.

Music Production

Lo-fi beats and ambient soundscapes in Ableton

Music production started as a way to unwind after coding sessions. I work in Ableton Live, layering field recordings with soft synths and vinyl crackle. A few tracks have found their way onto lo-fi playlists.

Open Source

Contributing to tools that make dev life better

I contribute to several open-source projects focused on developer tooling and accessibility. Most recently I've been working on a Figma-to-code plugin and a CLI tool for scaffolding design systems.

Cycling

Weekend century rides and bike-packing adventures

Road cycling is my moving meditation. I ride a Canyon Endurace and do regular century rides around the Mekong Delta. Last year I completed a 5-day bike-packing trip from Saigon to Dalat through the Central Highlands.
